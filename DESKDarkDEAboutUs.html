Our Journey Towards a Sustainable Future
Founded in 2018 by three passionate engineers, deMITasse Energies began with a vision to tackle one of the greatest challenges of our time: climate change. Our mission is to develop advanced long-duration energy storage solutions that make renewable energy a reliable and viable option for everyone. From our humble beginnings in a small lab, we've grown into a pioneering force in the energy sector, driven by innovation and a commitment to sustainability.
"We believe that renewable energy is the key to a sustainable future, and our mission is to unlock its full potential for generations to come."
- Alex Johnson, Co-Founder & CEO
At deMITasse Energies, we are not just building technology; we are building a movement. Our team is composed of experts from diverse fields, all united by a common goal: to create a cleaner, greener world. We pride ourselves on our inclusive culture, with over 40% of our team being women in STEM, including our co-founder. This diversity of thought and experience drives our creativity and innovation, allowing us to develop groundbreaking solutions that are both efficient and cost-effective.
Our journey is one of relentless pursuit of excellence. We've forged strong partnerships with leading companies across various industries, proving the reliability and scalability of our technology through successful pilot projects. We are committed to taking our solutions from the lab to the real world, ensuring that our technology is not just a concept, but a tangible force for good.
Our Team
“Passionate. Diverse. Driven by Sustainability”
At deMITasse Energies, our team is the cornerstone of our innovation and success. We are a diverse group of experts, united by a shared passion for sustainability and a commitment to solving the world's energy challenges. Our team includes engineers, scientists, and industry leaders, all dedicated to creating a cleaner, greener future through advanced energy storage solutions.
Our co-founders, Alex Johnson, Priya Sharma, and Maria Lopez, bring together decades of experience in energy storage technologies, engineering, and business leadership. Under their guidance, deMITasse Energies has grown from a small startup to a leading innovator in the renewable energy sector. With over 40% of our team being women in STEM, we pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and collaborative environment that drives creativity and excellence. Every member of our team is committed to our mission of making renewable energy a reliable and viable option for everyone, and their diverse perspectives and expertise are what make us strong. Join us on this journey as we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in clean energy.
